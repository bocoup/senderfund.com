The Sender Fund supports women in technology & business

*

It's brand new, and we're starting small & smart.

As an MIT Scholarship that provides financial support to women in engineering & entrepreneurship, we want to hit $200,000 in fundraising by the 2013 School year.

MIT is a great place for us to start. The Sloan school & the Entrepreneurship program & the engineering programs produce high performance people. We're changing tomorrow's landscape by investing in today's students.

This fund exists with support from Boaz Sender, Florence's son & a technology entrepreneur. He owns and operates Bocoup, an open web technology company.

How do I help?

use a credit card

Give to the Florence Sender Fund through the MIT Giving site.

On the MIT giving form, select in memory of, and enter Florence Sender as the Honoree. She is related to MIT as a faculty member.

If the fund does not appear, search for 'Sender'

send a check

Checks may be mailed to:

  • Bonny Kellermann '72
  • MIT Director of Memorial Gifts
  • 600 Memorial Drive, MIT Room W98-516
  • Cambridge, MA 02139
Check should be payable to MIT. They should indicate on check memo or cover note that their gift is in memory of Florence Sender.

be a volunteer

You can help by sharing the word. If you or someone you know is interested in supporting women in technology & entrepreneurship, put them in touch! Introduce us with an email to pete@senderfund.com.
